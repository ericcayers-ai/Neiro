# Neiro

## Product
Local audio worksuite for source separation, restoration, transcription, and editing. Everything runs on-device; the UI is a thin client over a local engine.

## Audience
Musicians, mixers, and learners who need stems, cleanup, MIDI, and practice tools without uploading audio.

## Register
product

## Platform
web (Tauri desktop shell + browser against the same local engine)

## Voice
Plain operational language. Progress and errors name the real stage and constraint — never marketing voice.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS purple gradients, Inter-only marketing kits, glassmorphism dashboards, nested card grids, side-stripe callout cards.
